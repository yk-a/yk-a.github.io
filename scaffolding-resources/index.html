A Study Into Scaffolding

About

This is a page of resources for A Study Into Scaffolding, a 2022 architecture diploma project at AHO. The actual diploma explores methods of repair through the secondary structures that facilitate this process - scaffolding.

Resources and Further Reading

References here. The diploma explores both the literal and figurative dimensions of scaffolding.
