2021maintenancepreservationresource

A Study into Scaffolding investigates scaffolding's relationship with the built environment. As a architecture diploma its focus was oriented towards presenting scaffolding's significance in an architectural context, but ideas of maintenance generally have applications beyond the scope of architecture.

Scaffolding has relevance in many contexts - this is a list of resources and further reading on scaffolding and maintenance that I used in my diploma. I hope it can be helpful to others interested in thinking about other kinds of essential systems and alternative ways to approach the field of architectural preservation.
